# Laboratory Guide

## Location
The Laboratory is on the Ground Floor of the Diagnostics Building, next to Radiology.

## Process
1. Bring your doctor's test prescription and Patient ID card to the Laboratory reception.
2. Sample collection (blood, urine, etc.) is done at the collection counter.
3. Most routine reports (CBC, blood sugar, lipid profile) are ready within 4-6 hours; specialized tests may take 24-48 hours.

## Fasting Requirements
Some tests, such as fasting blood sugar or lipid profile, require 8-12 hours of fasting. Check with your doctor or the Laboratory reception when scheduling.

## Report Collection
Reports can be collected in person at the Laboratory counter or accessed online via the patient portal, if registered.
