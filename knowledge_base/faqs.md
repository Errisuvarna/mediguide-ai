# Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I register as a new patient?**
A: Visit the Registration counter on the Ground Floor with a valid ID proof and address proof. Staff will create your patient file and issue a Patient ID card.

**Q: Where is the Emergency Department?**
A: Ground Floor of the Emergency Block, clearly marked with red signage. It operates 24x7.

**Q: How can I get an itemized bill?**
A: Submit a request at the Billing counter with your admission ID; itemized bills are usually ready within 24 hours.

**Q: Is cashless insurance accepted?**
A: Yes, for most major insurers. Present your policy card at the Insurance Desk at the time of admission.

**Q: Where is the Pharmacy?**
A: Ground Floor of the Main Building, next to the OPD waiting area.

**Q: What documents do I need for admission?**
A: ID proof, insurance card (if applicable), and the admission deposit receipt. Previous medical records help but aren't mandatory.

**Q: Can I get a wheelchair?**
A: Yes, wheelchairs are available free of charge at every building entrance and at the Reception desk.

**Q: Does the hospital have multilingual support?**
A: Yes, the AI assistant and many staff members support English, Hindi, and Telugu.
