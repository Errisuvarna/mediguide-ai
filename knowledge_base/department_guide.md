# Department Guide

## How to Find the Right Department
If you are unsure which department to visit, describe your main symptom or need at the Reception desk or to the AI assistant, and you will be directed accordingly.

## Common Departments
- **General Medicine** — everyday illness, fever, general checkups (Floor 1, Main Building)
- **Cardiology** — heart and chest-related concerns (Floor 3, Main Building)
- **Orthopedics** — bone, joint, and spine issues (Floor 2, Main Building)
- **Neurology** — brain and nerve-related issues, headaches, seizures (Floor 4, Main Building)
- **Pulmonology** — breathing difficulty, asthma, cough (Floor 3, Main Building)
- **Pediatrics** — children's healthcare (Floor 1, Main Building)
- **Gynecology** — women's health and maternity care (Floor 2, Main Building)
- **Emergency** — urgent or life-threatening conditions (Ground Floor, Emergency Block, 24x7)
- **Laboratory** — blood tests and diagnostics (Ground Floor, Diagnostics Building)
- **Radiology & Imaging** — X-ray, CT, MRI, ultrasound (Ground Floor, Diagnostics Building)
- **Pharmacy** — medicine dispensing (Ground Floor, Main Building)

## OPD Timings
Most departments run OPD from Monday to Saturday, 09:00 to 17:00. Emergency and Pharmacy operate 24x7.
