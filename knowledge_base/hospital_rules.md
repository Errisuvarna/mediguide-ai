# Hospital Rules & Visitor Policy

## Visiting Hours
General visiting hours are 11:00-13:00 and 17:00-19:00 daily. Only two visitors are permitted per patient at a time in general wards.

## ICU Visiting
ICU visiting is restricted to immediate family members and requires permission from nursing staff. Visiting slots are typically short (10-15 minutes) to protect patient recovery.

## Photography
Photography and video recording are not permitted in patient care areas without prior hospital administration approval, to protect patient privacy.

## Mobile Phones
Mobile phones should be kept on silent mode in wards and ICU areas.

## Parking
Multi-level parking is available near the Main Building entrance. A dedicated drop-off zone is reserved for Emergency vehicles and elderly or mobility-impaired patients.

## Smoking
The hospital campus is entirely smoke-free.
