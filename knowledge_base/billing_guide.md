# Billing Guide

## Overview
The Billing department handles all payments for consultations, procedures, admissions, and pharmacy purchases.

## Locations
The main Billing counter is on the Ground Floor of the Main Building, near the Admission counter. A separate quick-payment counter is available near the OPD for consultation fees only.

## Payment Methods
Cash, debit/credit cards, UPI, and net banking are all accepted at every billing counter.

## Itemized Bills
Patients or attendants can request an itemized bill breakdown at the Billing counter using their admission ID. Itemized bills are typically ready within 24 hours of request.

## Advance Payments
For scheduled surgeries and procedures, an advance payment is usually required at least one day before the procedure date.

## Refunds
If a deposit exceeds the final bill amount, the difference is refunded to the original payment method within 5-7 working days.
