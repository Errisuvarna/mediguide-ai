# Insurance Guide

## Overview
Most major insurance providers are accepted at the hospital through both cashless and reimbursement claim routes.

## Cashless Process
1. Present your insurance policy card at the Insurance Desk (Ground Floor, near Admission) at the time of admission.
2. The Insurance Desk verifies your policy and sends a pre-authorization request to your insurer.
3. Once approved, treatment proceeds and the insurer settles the bill directly with the hospital, minus any co-payment or non-covered items.

## Reimbursement Process
If cashless isn't available for your policy, you pay the hospital directly and then submit the discharge summary, itemized bill, and payment receipts to your insurer for reimbursement.

## Documents Required
- Insurance Policy Card
- Government ID Proof
- Pre-authorization Form (for planned admissions)
- Claim Form
- Hospital Empanelment Letter (if requested by your insurer)

## Important Notes
Coverage varies by policy — always confirm what is covered with the Insurance Desk before a planned admission. Emergency admissions proceed with treatment first, and insurance formalities are completed alongside.
