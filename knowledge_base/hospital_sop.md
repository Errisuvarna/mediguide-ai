# Hospital Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) Summary

## Patient Registration SOP
New patients register at the Ground Floor Registration counter with a government ID and address proof. A unique Patient ID is issued and used for all future visits.

## Emergency Response SOP
Any patient arriving with symptoms of chest pain, severe bleeding, breathing difficulty, unconsciousness, or suspected stroke is triaged immediately at the Emergency Department without requiring prior registration formalities.

## Consultation SOP
Patients are called in order of their token number at each department's OPD. Token numbers are issued at the department reception or Registration counter.

## Infection Control SOP
Hand sanitization stations are placed at every entrance and department. Visitors with active respiratory symptoms are asked to wear a mask.

## Data Privacy SOP
Patient records are confidential and accessible only to treating doctors, authorized nursing staff, and the patient (or authorized attendant) through the patient portal.
